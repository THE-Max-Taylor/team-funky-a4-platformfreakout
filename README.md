HOW TO PLAY: Kevin McCalister has ventured to a land beyond space, time, or Christmas itself. An ominous cube rests on the horizon. Kevin MUST attempt to claim it if he is to return home. He must then venture through a realm composed of switches, wood panelling, and lethality, before traversing the planes of ice and death. Is there any hope of escape? Shall he ever get out? Stay tuned and you shall see...
